**African Mango** — Functions as a fat-burning agent and has also been studied for its effect on blood lipids. Some research suggests it may support leptin sensitivity, which is relevant for appetite regulation.

**Guarana** — Provides a mild metabolic stimulation. Unlike straight caffeine, guarana releases more slowly and tends to provide sustained energy rather than a sharp peak followed by a drop.
